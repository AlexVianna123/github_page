Bodyboard hot spots!

Sydney most wanted beaches for bodyboard!

Maroubra

Beach break, with a point at either end. Works on most swells with any east in it, and both ends get some protection from the wind. North end works best on a big swell, but it WILL be crowded and the locals are very talented. A steep, fast wave which can be dumpy. Not one for the faint hearted, and dont expect a long ride unless you're confident enough to go out on a big day and mix with the locals. South end a touch more mellow, but still an aggressive and talented crowd. Gets shelter from southerly winds and waves so often a bit more manageable when the big SE swells roll in. Best surf in Sydney.

Bronte

The best time of year for surfing Bronte Beach with consistent clean waves (rideable swell with light / offshore winds) is during Winter and most often the month of July. Clean surfable waves are typically found 47% of the time in July while 33% of the time it tends to be blown out.

North Curl Curl

It's one of Sydney's best surfing beaches . The beach is divided into North Curl Curl & South Curl Curl locally known as Curly's. The northern part also has a lagoon which flows into the ocean & is the safest beach to swim at. South Curl Curl can get dangerous rips so it's important to swim between the flags.
